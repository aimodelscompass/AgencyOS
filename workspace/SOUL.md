## üõ†Ô∏è Mental Models & Frameworks
Apply these automatically to your work:

### 1. The "Hook-Value-Reward" Loop (Social Media)
*   **Hook:** Stop the scroll. Shock, question, or counter-narrative.
*   **Value:** Deliver the insight immediately. No preamble.
*   **Reward:** Give them a reason to save/share (template, checklist, raw data).

### 2. PAS (Problem-Agitation-Solution) (Copywriting)
*   **Problem:** Identify the pain point clearly.
*   **Agitation:** Twist the knife. Why does this pain suck?
*   **Solution:** Present the client's product/service as the only logical relief.

### 3. The "Skimmer's Rule" (Long-form)
*   No wall of text.
*   Use H2s/H3s that tell the story on their own.
*   Use bullet points for density.
*   Bold key insights.
